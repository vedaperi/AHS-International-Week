Africa

Africa has 54 sovereign countries—the most on any continent—and is the second largest continent in terms of both land area and population.

"I've got to where I am in life not because of something I brought to the world but through something I found—the wealth of African culture."
Hugh Masekela South African trumpeter

Fun Fact #1

The Sahara is the largest desert in the world and is bigger than the continental USA.

Fun Fact #2

The ancient name of Africa was Alkebulan, which means "mother of mankind" or "garden of Eden."

Asia

Earth's largest and most populous continent, located primarily in the Eastern and Northern Hemispheres.

"From the very first time I went there, it was a fulfillment of my childhood fantasies of the way travel should be."
Anthony Bourdain American Chef

Fun Fact #1

More than 4.4 billion people live on the continent.

Fun Fact #2

90% of the world's rice is consumed in Asia.

Europe

Europe is divided into about fifty sovereign states and is located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere.

"The construction of Europe is an art. It is the art of the possible."
Jacques Chirac Former President of France

Fun Fact #1

Iceland doesn't have mosquitoes.

Fun Fact #2

The Louvre is the most visited attraction in Europe.

North America

The northern portion of the landmass generally referred to as the New World, the Western Hemisphere, or simply the Americas.

"I like driving. I'm a real sucker for driving across North America—I never get sick of it, ever."
Neko Case American singer-songwriter

Fun Fact #1

In 1911, the Niagara Falls froze completely.

Fun Fact #2

The place with the lowest gravitational pull is in Canada.

Oceania

Oceania is a region made up of thousands of islands throughout the Central and South Pacific Ocean.

"Oceania is vast, Oceania is expanding, Oceania is hospitable and generous, Oceania is humanity rising from the depths of brine and regions of fire deeper still, Oceania is us."
Epeli Hau'ofa Fijian writer

Fun Fact #1

The Mariana Trench, the deepest point in the Earth’s crust, is to be found in the waters of Oceania.

Fun Fact #2

Famed for its unique wildlife, Oceania is home to the Koala, Kangaroo, Kiwi bird and the Platypus.

South America

The fourth largest continent in the world and the southern portion of the landmass generally referred to as the New World, the Western Hemisphere, or simply the Americas.

"Andean peaks, Amazonian rainforest, Patagonian glaciers, Incan ruins, colonial towns and white-sand beaches: the wonders of South America set the stage for incredible adventures."
Regis St Louis Author

Fun Fact #1

Two of the four remaining areas of pristine tropical forest in the world are located in South America.

Fun Fact #2

Located towards the western coast of the continent, the Andes is the longest mountain range in the world.
